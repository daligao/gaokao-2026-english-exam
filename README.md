## What is this?

The **Gaokao (高考)** is China's national college entrance examination — the single test that determines which university a student can attend. For most students, it's the most important day of their life, shaped by years of preparation.

This tool is the **real 2026 English reading section**, converted into an interactive format. 20 questions from the actual paper sat by 12.6 million students. No edits. No simplification.
